Meet Our Doctors

Dr. Sanjay Sachdeva – Otorhinolaryngologist

Sanjay is one of the best ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat) Surgeons in Delhi with a specialization in Otorhinolaryngology. Otorhinolaryngology is a surgical subspecialty within medicine that deals with the surgical and medical management of conditions of the head and neck. He is recognized as an expert in the area of Endoscopic Minimally Invasive Approaches for Skull Base Surgery. Sanjay has conducted several live surgery workshops and has been an invited faculty, moderator, and speaker at many national and international conferences. He has received several awards and accolades for his remarkable contributions to the field of Medicine, including an award from The Association of Otolaryngology of India for his outstanding work.

Anita Bhardwaj- High Altitude Rescue Doctor

Anita is a joint medical director of the Six Sigma High Altitude Medical Rescue Services and she has received the highest award, the Nari Shakti Puraskar, for women in India. The service has saved the lives of over 5,000 women and children operating at altitudes up to 24,000 feet. Anita was awarded the Kalpana Chawla Shaurya Award in 2014. She was later awarded the Nari Shakti Puraskar was made in the Durbar room of the Rashtrapati Bhavan by the President of India on International Women’s Day. It is the highest civilian award for women.

Dr. Naresh Trehan – cardiothoracic surgeon

Trehan is an Indian cardiovascular and cardiothoracic surgeon. He graduated from King George Medical College, Lucknow, India, and went on to practice at New York University Medical Center, Manhattan USA from 1971 to 1988. Later he returned to India and started Escorts Heart Institute and Research Center. As chairman of Global Health Private Limited, Trehan has overseen the building of an integrated healthcare facility in Gurgaon, India, referred to as Medanta The Medicity. Medicine combines modern medicine with traditional medicine and holistic therapies. Trehan serves as the chairman, managing director, and chief cardiac surgeon of Medanta. He has also served as a personal surgeon to the President of India since 1991 and has received numerous awards in the course of his career.

Dr. Surbhi Anand – Endodontist

After completing her bachelor’s degree, she pursued a master’s course in Endodontology. She is well known for specializing in Single sitting Root Canal Treatment, cosmetic dentistry, and giving a smile makeover. She has been associated as a senior consultant with healthcare setups including Fortis Hospital and Columbia Asia Hospital and has a vast consultation base to various reputable dental practices in Delhi/NCR. In her career, Surbhi has published more than half a dozen international and national research publications. She was also conferred with the best scientific paper presentation award at an international conference held in Dubai. She is a firm believer in rendering quality dental care with compassion.
